Lincoln Center Econonomics Society

Our Mission

To empower students by advancing economic understanding, fostering analytical debate, and providing direct access to industry leaders. Through skill-building initiatives, impactful publications, and strategic partnerships, we bridge the academic and professional worlds to shape future economists and informed leaders.

About Us

The Econ Society is a student-led organization committed to advancing economic understanding, fostering debate, and connecting students with industry leaders. We provide an engaging platform for students to explore complex economic topics, strengthen their analytical skills, and develop a deeper appreciation for the role economics plays in shaping our world.
